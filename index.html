Our Expertise

We have hundreds of expert potters employed in our production factory. All our products are handmade by our potters and are not imported. They mould the clay manually into various shapes and take it to the workers in the kiln section for heating up.

Our Varieties

We deal with all kinds of ceramics which are always available for purchase. You can easily visit our shop section to order yours.

Earthenware:

Terracotta, Redware (American), Victorian Majolica, Lusterware, Raku, Ironstone (China) & Yellowware.

Stoneware:

Fine Stoneware, Traditional Stoneware, Chemical Stoneware, Electrical Stoneware & Thermo Shock-resistant Stoneware.

Porcelain:

Hard-paste Porcelain, Soft-paste Porcelain & Bone China.
